Virtual Study Ke , A virtual study app where students can meet and discuss syllabus, review exam papers and content promoted via study tools, discussions, help, guidelines and free study material.

As people are encouraged to stay a home, schools forced to close and students stay home, this requires students to continue learning from home. As you know this is not possible without online study material. That is where Virtual Study comes in.
